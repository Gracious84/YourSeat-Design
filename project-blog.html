Montgomery County Increasing Parenting Skills of At-risk Children
Last updated April 24, 2019
In Progress

In early 2019, Montgomery County Social Services agreed to serve as YourSeat’s pilot customer to measure participants for client behavior change in their R.E.A.L. Parenting program; aimed at helping parents - in jeopardy of losing child custody- retool their parenting skills. The pilot program aimed to study participants for 12 months to determine whether clients improved after attending the program.
