Reported Issues

Solution

We know your team is already pressed for time and resources. Adding another item to your team's already busy plate feels wrong.

Our team specializes in creating Family First compliant measurement material, and handles the heavy lifting to ensure ongoing measurement of behavior change occurs.

REPORTING

Pre-program to 1 year follow-up surveys and reporting are done for you, along with creating complex research design experiments to ensure your programming is working

TECHNOLOGY

Data analysis is performed inside our technology to detect clients not improving, why a client's improving, and when another behavior change check-in is needed

STAFF

A human touch is always there checking in on your team and make sure client feedback is being collected, as well as answer more complex research questions along the way
