Frequently asked questions

Why was the name ‘YourSeat’ chosen?

It reflects our commitment to seeing problems from your perspective, from your seat; not ours. We are here to listen

Where is the organization located?

YourSeat is headquartered in Washington, D.C.

Who is YourSeat going to help?

Our focus is Social Services Departments currently operating evidence-based programming in areas such as Family Stabilization, Home Visiting Prevention, Interventions for Neglect and Parenting Training Programs that Address Child Abuse/Neglect

How is YourSeat going to improve measuring client behavior change?

After learning about the program Social Services Departments wish to measure, our team creates the necessary documents, such as multiple surveys, program evaluations manual & multiple reports and a quasi-experimentation plan, to measure the selected program for client behavior change. All survey data is stored in our technology that detects clients not improving and alerts your team. The technology also contains features to alert the clients’ provider
